Endianess

0x 00 00 00 01

if we consider the leftmost byte to be the most significant, meaning a big-endian number, that would yield the value

00 * 2^24 + 00 * 2^16 + 00 * 2^8 + 01 * 2^0 = 1

big-endian stores the numbers the same way humans write them

but if we consider the rightmost byte to be the most significant, meaning a little-endian number, that would instead yield the value

00 * 2^0 + 00 * 2^8 + 00 * 2^16 + 01 * 2^24 = 2^24
